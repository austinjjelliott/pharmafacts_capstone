Free Information On FDA-Approved Medications

Welcome to PharmaFacts, where you can find FDA-approved information on both prescription and over-the-counter medications. We utilize the free openFDA API to ensure you receive the most current information available. Please note that the content provided here is for informational purposes only and should not be considered medical advice. For any health concerns, we encourage you to consult with your healthcare provider. In case of an emergency, please call 911 and seek immediate assistance.
